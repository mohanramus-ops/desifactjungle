Public Interest Litigation (PIL) Meaning In Hindi

December 5, 2025

PIL meaning kya hai? with Examples¬†

Public Interest Litigation (PIL) ek aisa legal process hai jisme koi bhi citizen court me case file kar sakta hai public interest, yaani janhit ke liye. PIL ka use tab hota hai jab issue poore samaj, community, environment, ya public rights ko affect karta ho. Isme personal nuksan hona zaroori nahi hota.

PIL kyon use kiya jata hai?

PIL ka purpose ye hota hai ki aam log bhi bina kisi personal interest ke public ke bhale ke liye case file kar saken. Court isse social justice ko promote karti hai aur un issues par action leti hai jahan government ya authorities fail ho rahi hoti hain.

PIL kab file ki ja sakti hai?

PIL file ki ja sakti hai jab:

  • Public ko nuksan ho raha ho

  • Environment damage ho raha ho

  • Government apna duty theek se nahi kar rahi ho

  • Public resources misuse ho rahe ho

  • Human rights violate ho rahe ho

  • Poor ya helpless log apna case file nahi kar pa rahe ho

PIL ke examples

Example 1: Pollution issue

Agar kisi city me pollution bahut badh gaya ho aur authority action nahi le rahi ho, to koi bhi citizen iske khilaf PIL file kar sakta hai.

Example 2: Illegal construction

Agar koi illegal building ban rahi ho jo public safety ko damage kar sakti ho, to public interest me PIL file ki ja sakti hai.

Example 3: Environmental protection

Jungle cutting, river pollution, wildlife harm jaise issues par PIL file ki ja sakti hai.

Example 4: Poor people ke rights

Jahaan gareeb ya marginalized people apna case khud nahi file kar sakte, koi activist ya organization unke behalf me PIL file kar sakti hai.
