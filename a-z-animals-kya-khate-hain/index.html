Animals ka food kaise decide hota hai?

Animals ka khaana unke dant, pet ki capacity, aur jeevan shaili par depend karta hai. Kuch animals sirf paudhe khate hain (herbivore), kuch sirf maans khate hain (carnivore), aur kuch dono (omnivore).

Herbivore Animals Kya Khate Hain? (Plant-eating)

Herbivores examples

Cow, goat, buffalo, rabbit, horse, sheep

Kya khate hain?

  • Grass

  • Green leaves

  • Vegetables

  • Fruits

  • Grains

  • Hay

Carnivore Animals Kya Khate Hain? (Meat-eating)

Carnivores examples

Lion, tiger, wolf, owl, jackal

Kya khate hain?

  • Meat

  • Fish

  • Chhote animals

  • Birds

  • Insects

Omnivore Animals Kya Khate Hain? (Both plant + meat)

Omnivores examples

Bear, monkey, pig, human

Kya khate hain?

  • Fruits

  • Seeds

  • Insects

  • Meat

  • Plants

FAQs

1. Kaun sa animal sabse zyada khata hai?

Elephant sabse zyada plant-based food khaata hai.

2. Kaun sa animal sirf maans khata hai?

Lion, tiger, vulture jaise animals pure carnivore hote hain.

3. Rabbit kya khaata hai?

Rabbit grass, leaves, carrot aur green fodder khata hai.

4. Monkey kya khata hai?

Monkey fruits, insects aur nuts khata hai.

5. Fish kya khati hai?

Fish algae, chhote insects aur aquatic worms khati hai.
