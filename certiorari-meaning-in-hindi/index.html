Certiorari kya hota hai?

Certiorari ek mahatvapurna constitutional writ hoti hai jise High Court ya Supreme Court jari karti hai. Is writ ka purpose hota hai kisi lower court ya tribunal ke illegal decision ko cancel karna, ya unki proceedings ko higher court ke paas transfer karna.

Saral shabdon me ‚Äî agar lower court ne galat tareeke se verdict diya ho, ya apne jurisdiction ke bahar jaake decision diya ho, to Certiorari writ se us decision ko quash (radd) kiya ja sakta hai.

Is writ ka aim hota hai judicial errors ko sudharna aur fair justice ensure karna.

Certiorari writ kab use hoti hai?

Certiorari writ tab use hoti hai jab:

  • Lower court apne jurisdiction se bahar decision de

  • Tribunal illegal tareeke se case decide kare

  • Natural justice ka violation ho

  • Court biased decision de

  • Court ne procedure follow na ki ho

  • Evidence bina authority ke use kiya ho

Certiorari ke examples

Example 1

Agar ek tribunal aise matter par decision de jisme uske paas jurisdiction hi nahi tha, to High Court Certiorari se us decision ko radd kar sakti hai.

Example 2

Agar lower court ne bina proper hearing diye kisi party ke khilaaf decision de diya, to Supreme Court Certiorari writ jari kar sakti hai.

Example 3

Agar koi court biased ho aur natural justice follow na kare, to higher court uske pura process ko cancel kar deti hai.

Certiorari writ ka uddeshya

Certiorari writ ka main purpose hota hai:

  • Lower courts ke wrong decisions ko quash karna

  • Illegal proceedings ko rokna

  • Judicial fairness ensure karna

  • Public ke legal rights ko protect karna

  • Courts ko unke jurisdiction me rakhna
