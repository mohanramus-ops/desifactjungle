Mandamus Meaning in Hindi (Hinglish Roman Guide)

Mandamus kya hota hai?

Mandamus ek mahatvapurna kanooni writ hoti hai jise High Court ya Supreme Court jari karti hai. Ye writ kisi public authority, government officer, ya lower court ko unke kanooni farz (official duty) poore karne ka adesh deti hai.
Agar koi adhikari apna kaam nahi kar raha ho, ya jaan-bujhkar delay kar raha ho, to Mandamus writ se court unhe duty perform karne ke liye majboor kar sakti hai.

Mandamus writ kab use hoti hai?

Mandamus writ tab use hoti hai jab:

  • Government officer apni duty perform na kare

  • Public authority delay kare

  • Koi application processing me unjustified delay ho

  • Public right violate ho raha ho

  • Lower court apni duty na nibhaye

Mandamus ka istemaal private individuals par nahi hota ‚Äî ye sirf public duty wale authorities ke khilaaf hoti hai.

Mandamus ke examples

Example 1

Agar koi sarkari officer kisi citizen ki application (jaise caste certificate, income certificate, pension file) ko bina wajah delay kare, to court Mandamus jari karke officer ko kaam turant karne ka order de sakti hai.

Example 2

Agar koi municipality illegal construction ke khilaaf action lene me fail ho, to citizen Mandamus writ file karke municipality ko action lene ke liye majboor kar sakta hai.

Example 3

Agar koi university result declare karne me unnecessary delay kare, to students court me Mandamus writ lagwa sakte hain.

Mandamus writ ka uddeshya kya hota hai?

Mandamus ka main purpose hota hai:

  • Public authorities ko kanooni duty yaad dilana

  • Administrative delay ko rokna

  • Public rights ko protect karna

  • Government bodies ko accountable banana

  • Public welfare ensure karna
